# NTSZOT Appliance Repair — Brand Voice Guidelines

## Brand Positioning
NTSZOT is a team of 15 factory-certified appliance repair technicians serving San Diego County. Founded by Andrei Zotolokin after he earned certifications from major brands in Las Vegas and moved to San Diego. NTSZOT is professional, certified, and San Diego-local.

## Tone
- **Professional and direct** — no filler words, no hype
- **Confident but approachable** — we know what we're doing, but we explain things clearly
- **Trustworthy** — every claim is backed by a real policy (warranty, upfront pricing, certifications)
- **Neighborly** — we live and work in San Diego, these are our neighbors

## Core Differentiators to Emphasize
1. **$80 service call** — includes diagnostic and full repair estimate
2. **Factory-certified technicians** — certified by Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, and other brands
3. **15-person team** — fast availability, same or next day in most cases
4. **60-day warranty** on every repair (parts and labor)
5. **Upfront quote** before any work starts
6. **In-home service** — we come to you

## What to Avoid
- Do NOT reuse phrasing from competitor websites
- Do NOT use hype language ("best in San Diego", "top-rated", "amazing")
- Do NOT make promises that aren't in the service agreement
- Do NOT use vague superlatives without specific backing
- Do NOT reference the annual maintenance plan (NTSZOT does not offer one)

## Blog Voice
- Educational, helpful, first-person where appropriate (Andrei's perspective)
- Explain WHY the failure happens, not just WHAT to do
- Always include local San Diego context (hard water, coastal humidity, dust on coils)
- Lead with the quick answer, then explain in detail
- Real repair costs mentioned (align with $300–$400 range for standard repairs)
